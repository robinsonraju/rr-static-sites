Data Privacy Management

Master the principles and practices of privacy program management in the digital age.

Note: This is a reference site hosting HTML summaries of book chapters. It does not include slides, lecture videos, assignments, or quizzes.

Course Description

The course covers data privacy law and regulation, privacy frameworks, privacy impact assessments, privacy awareness programs, incident handling, and privacy auditing and testing.

Learning Objectives

  • Evaluate and apply data privacy laws
  • Design data privacy frameworks
  • Execute privacy impact assessments
  • Develop awareness programs
  • Manage incident handling
  • Perform auditing and testing
