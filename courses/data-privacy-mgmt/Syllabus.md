# Data Privacy Management
Master the principles and practices of privacy program management in the digital age.

This is a reference site to host a portion of the course materials for INFA 722 - Data Privacy Management. It mainly includes links to HTML summaries of the book chapters covered in the course. Does not include slides, lecture videos, assignments, quizzes, discussions or other core course materials.

## Course Description
The course covers data privacy law and regulation, privacy frameworks, privacy impact assessments, privacy awareness programs, incident handling, and privacy auditing and testing.

## Course Objectives
Students should be able to:
* Evaluate and apply data privacy laws and regulations
* Design data privacy frameworks
* Create and execute privacy impact assessments
* Develop data privacy awareness programs
* Manage data privacy incident handling
* Perform data privacy auditing and testing
* Conduct independent data privacy research
